Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What types of aluminum wires and cables do we offer?
We offer a wide range of aluminum wires and cables, including single-core, multi-core, insulated, bare conductors, and specialized aluminum alloy cables for various industries such as electrical, telecommunications, and construction. Our products are designed to meet international standards and are customizable to fit specific project requirements.
What makes our copper wire different from other suppliers?
Our copper wire is made from 99.99% pure electrolytic copper, ensuring superior conductivity and minimal resistance. This high-grade purity means our wires provide excellent durability and better energy efficiency, reducing power loss in transmission compared to lower-quality alternatives.
What sets our cable insulation apart from others?
We use high-quality, eco-friendly insulation materials that not only offer superior heat resistance and durability but also ensure excellent flexibility. This makes our cables easier to handle during installation without compromising safety or longevity. Our insulation also has excellent moisture and UV resistance, adding to the lifespan of the product.
What type of quality assurance do we offer?
Every batch of wire undergoes strict quality testing, including tensile strength, conductivity, and corrosion resistance. We adhere to international industry standards, and our products come with detailed certifications, ensuring reliability and safety for all applications.
What type of variety we offer?
We offer more then 15 different products which are applicable at any demand sector. Our products are flexible , as they are dependent on the customar usage.
What distinguishes our wires' flexibility for installation??
Our wires are designed with a higher strand count, allowing for greater flexibility, easier installation in tight spaces, and reduced risk of breakage compared to stiffer, low-strand wires.
How do our cables perform under extreme weather conditions?
Our cables are tested for resistance to extreme temperatures, UV exposure, and moisture, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications, unlike many competitors' cables that degrade faster under harsh conditions.
Why are our cables less prone to corrosion??
Our wires and cables are treated with anti-corrosive coatings and designed for optimal resistance against environmental factors like humidity and oxidation, which extends their lifespan significantly beyond the average.
How does our production differ from others?
Our raw material is transported from Germany and it goes through multiple layers of inspections. After that is undergoes multiple test to check its durability , efficiency and security .
What is the lifespan of our copper and aluminum wires compared to others??
Due to superior materials and manufacturing processes, our wires and cables have a longer lifespan, often lasting 20-30% longer than those of competitors, reducing the need for frequent replacements.
